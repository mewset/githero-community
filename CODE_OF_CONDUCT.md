# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want the GitHero community to be welcoming and inclusive for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Be excellent to each other:**

- Use welcoming and friendly language
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Show empathy towards others

**Not acceptable:**

- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Community leaders will remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Repeated or serious violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please [open an issue](https://github.com/mewset/githero-community/issues/new) or contact the maintainers directly.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).
